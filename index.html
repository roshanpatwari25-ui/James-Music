Crafting sonic landscapes that blur the lines between intimacy and grandeur.

With a foundation in classical guitar and a voice that traverses three octaves, Elara creates music that exists in the liminal space between genres. Not quite folk, not quite jazz, but something entirely her own.

Having shared stages with artists like Nick Cave and Joni Mitchell, her approach combines technical precision with raw emotional vulnerability. Each performance is a journey—an exploration of silence as much as sound.
